A delicious and refreshing homemade raspberry yogurt ice cream recipe. Made with just a few simple ingredients, this creamy treat is perfect for summer!

Ingredients:

Slaw

  • 2 heads broccoli
  • 100g raisins
  • 100g roasted almonds, chopped

Dressing

  • 57g mayonnaise
  • 57g fat-free Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 tsp salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Peel off the tough outer layer of the broccoli stem using a vegetable peeler, then grate broccoli. You may also use a food processor or a box grater for grating.
  2. Make the dressing by combining all the dressing ingredients. Taste and adjust seasoning according to your preference.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, add shredded broccoli, raisins, and chopped almonds. Toss to combine.
  4. Drizzle dressing all over and toss until well combined.
  5. For the best result, let broccoli slaw sit in the fridge for at least 30 minutes for the flavors to meld before serving.
