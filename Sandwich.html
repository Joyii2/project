A delicious and refreshing homemade raspberry yogurt ice cream recipe. Made with just a few simple ingredients, this creamy treat is perfect for summer!

Ingredients:

  • 200g firm tofu
  • 1 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 1 tbsp tahini
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1.5 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp black salt
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper
  • Pinch of black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Drain the tofu and pat it dry with a paper towel.
  2. Crumble the tofu into a large mixing bowl with your hands until it reaches a "smashed egg" consistency.
  3. Add all the other ingredients listed above to the mixing bowl.
  4. Mix the ingredients until thoroughly combined.
  5. Assemble the sandwich by layering one slice of bread, followed by lettuce, vegan curried egg salad, and then the second slice of bread.
  6. Enjoy your Vegan Curried Tofu Salad Sandwich!
