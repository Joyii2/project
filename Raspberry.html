A delicious and refreshing homemade raspberry yogurt ice cream recipe. Made with just a few simple ingredients, this creamy treat is perfect for summer!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups frozen raspberries
  • 2 cups Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine frozen raspberries, Greek yogurt, honey, and vanilla extract.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the mixture into a loaf pan lined with parchment paper.
  4. Smooth the top with a spatula.
  5. Place the loaf pan in the freezer and freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm.
  6. Serve the raspberry yogurt ice cream in bowls and enjoy!
